Overview

Hoag's Object currently offers one excursion each in the planets of the Hazo and Ur. Excursions take place in and around the regions of Hazo and Ur's respective capital cities - Bethel and Can-dusa.

Galaxy Engine

The enigmatic galaxy engine of Hoag's Object is hidden away in the Xu'in mountains. Numerous travellers, pilgrims and locals travel here often.

Because the galaxy engine drives and sustains the galaxy, this is often a meeting point of many diverse life-forms. To many, it is a sacred ground and a place of worship. As such, a trip here is often a special occasion.

As a partner of the United Nations Initiative for Intergalactic Settlement (UNIIS), subsidies are offered on a qualification basis for those who do plan to travel and settle permanently. The cost of any trip made before the last trip in which travellers settle are borne by the travellers themselves. If travellers decide to settle permanently upon subsequent trips, they will receive a reimbursement upon confirmation of their settlement.
